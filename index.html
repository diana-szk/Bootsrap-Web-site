Who we are

A story
about us

Archi-esthetic was founded in 2011 with a commitment to making cities better places to live.We believe that architecture and design matter, and that through our work, we can make a positive difference in the world.

With over 200 architects and designers in New York, San Francisco, Boston, and Hong Kong, our work encompasses projects like the National September 11 Memorial, urban infrastructure and master planning projects, high-density mixed-use developments, multi-family projects across all income bands, hotels and corporate headquarters.
